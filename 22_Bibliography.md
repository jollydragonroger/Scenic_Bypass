# ABOUT THE AUTHOR

**Michael Laurence Curzi** is an independent researcher working at the intersection of physics, philosophy, and consciousness studies. His work seeks to bridge the gap between scientific rigor and contemplative wisdom, developing formal frameworks that include the observer in the description of reality.

This dissertation represents the culmination of years of inquiry into the fundamental nature of reality, informed by both Western scientific tradition and contemplative practice.
